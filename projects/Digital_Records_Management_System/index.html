Security & Compliance

Our Digital Records Management System is designed with top-notch security features and compliance measures to protect your data.

  • Data Encryption: All records are encrypted both in transit and at rest to ensure data confidentiality.
  • Access Controls: Role-based access controls allow you to manage who can view or edit specific records.
  • Audit Trails: Comprehensive logs of all record activities help you monitor access and changes for compliance.
  • Regulatory Compliance: Our system is designed to help you meet industry standards such as GDPR, HIPAA, and more.
